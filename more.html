My Story

Where I'm
coming from

I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Communication and Computer Science at the University of Illinois Chicago a dual focus that lets me think about technology not just as a technical challenge, but as a human.

My journey into tech started with curiosity. I wanted to understand how the apps I used every day were built, how algorithms made decisions, and how design shaped the way people felt when they used software. That curiosity turned into a passion.

Outside the classroom, I've built AI-powered applications, supported hundreds of students and faculty as a Computer Specialist, and mentored the next generation of women in tech through Girls Who Code and Break Through Tech.

01

Curiosity first

I ask "why" before "how" understanding the problem deeply before reaching for a solution.

02

Technology with Purpose

Technology should solve real problems. I focus on creating tools and systems that are useful, reliable, and meaningful for the people who use them.

03

I actively embrace learning new tools, frameworks, and workflows, understanding that growth comes from stepping outside my comfort zone.

04

Growth Mindset

I approach every project as a chance to improve—refining skills, learning new techniques, and building stronger solutions over time.
